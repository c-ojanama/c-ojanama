Projects

My most notable completed project to date is the creation of a multilingual large language model (LLM) that integrates OpenAI’s Whisper for speech recognition and a custom-trained BERT model for instruction interpretation and response generation.

This system was designed to understand commands in multiple languages, translate them into English, and provide both the translated question and answer in English alongside the user’s original language. The BERT model was fine-tuned on over 12,000 training commands, optimizing it for real-time multilingual comprehension and response accuracy.

The project involved developing and testing a full audio pipeline — from capturing microphone input to transcribing speech with Whisper, tokenizing inputs for BERT, and classifying or generating responses. The final product successfully demonstrated multilingual voice-to-text understanding and AI-driven reasoning, forming the foundation for a universal voice assistant framework.

I’m continuing to expand this work into related areas such as natural language understanding, robotics integration, and intelligent automation.
